The NetCDF plugin reads structure and trajectory data written as NetCDF files, by AMBER 9 and MMTK.

Notes:

  • The routines for reading AMBER 9 NetCDF files accept a subset of the possible combinations of data fields. In particular, VMD doesn't currently read the AMBER velocity data.
  • The MMTK routines currently parse atom data with a simple method that ignores atom grouping information and other high level organization stored in the "description" data.
